Welcome to Blogs from **Study With Me**. We are a collaborative study session tool *(in development)*. We aim to provide a shared space where friends can collaborate, study and improve themselves.

## Objectives

The objectives that we will follow are:

1. Providing a collaborative study space for students
2. Providing conferencing support
3. Providing whiteboard support for students to share their ideas.
4. Providing simulations for simple concepts making it easy for students to understand concepts easily.
