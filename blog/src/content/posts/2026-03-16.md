## Welcome to our WebRTC Demonstration

#### Published on: 2026-03-16

## **What have we done**: 

- **Firebase Storage Creation:** Temporary stores call offers, answers, and ICE candidates.
- **Simple UI Creation:** Streaming the video on both Local and Remote.
- **Call Functional Components:** Creating a new call, Join a call, Hangup the call.
- **Cloudflare Hosting:** Hosting static websites, such as HTML, CSS, and Jamstack sites, utilizing its global edge network for fast loading times.
